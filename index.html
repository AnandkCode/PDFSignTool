How to Sign PDF Documents Online

1

Upload Your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" and choose your document. Your file stays private and never leaves your device.

2

Create Signature

Draw your signature using your mouse or touchscreen. Adjust the size using the slider.

3

Place Signature

Click where you want to add your signature in the document. Drag to adjust position and size.

4

Download Signed PDF

Click "Download" to save your signed PDF. Your document is ready to use!
